3.2 Hospitality Calculations (Approach 1: blended ADR per season)
Let:
TotalRooms = Σ(roomType.rooms)
For each season s:
AvailableRoomNights_s = TotalRooms × NightsInSeason_s
OccupiedRoomNights_s = AvailableRoomNights_s × (Occupancy%_s / 100)
SeasonRevenue_s = OccupiedRoomNights_s × RatePerNight_s

AnnualRoomRevenue = Σ(SeasonRevenue_s)
EGI_used (hospitality) = AnnualRoomRevenue + OtherAnnualIncome

NOI = EGI_used − Opex + UtilityAdj
ValueRange:
ValueHigh = (NOI ÷ capLow) + ExcessLand − Refurb
ValueLow  = (NOI ÷ capHigh) + ExcessLand − Refurb
Display range low–high.

3.3 Hospitality Outputs
- Total Rooms
- Revenue per season (table)
- Total annual room revenue
- Total EGI used (annual)
- NOI
- Value range
- Optional: Modelled ADR (weighted) and Weighted Average Occupancy (weighted by room nights)

Weighted ADR:
WeightedADR = AnnualRoomRevenue ÷ Σ(OccupiedRoomNights_s)
Weighted Occupancy:
WeightedOcc = Σ(OccupiedRoomNights_s) ÷ Σ(AvailableRoomNights_s)
